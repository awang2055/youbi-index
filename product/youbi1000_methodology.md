# Youbi 1000 Methodology

## Overview
The Youbi 1000 is built on transparent, verifiable data.

We rank creators based on real engagement, real fan relationships, and sustainable growth — not vanity metrics.

---

## Engagement Score
We calculate a composite score from:
- paid messages
- gifts and tips
- subscriptions
- meaningful fan interactions

---

## Real Fan Metrics
We prioritize depth over breadth.

A creator with 50K highly engaged supporters can outrank one with 5M passive followers.

---

## Anti-Gaming Measures
We detect and penalize:
- bot activity
- fake engagement
- manipulation attempts

We audit continuously.

---

## Update Schedule
- Weekly updates
- Every Monday at 00:00 UTC
- Full historical tracking preserved

---

## Multi-Platform Data
We aggregate signals from:
- Youbi
- TikTok
- Instagram
- YouTube

---

## Tier Classification
- Diamond (Top 10)
- Gold (Top 100)
- Silver (Top 500)
- Bronze (Top 1000)

---

## Scoring Breakdown

| Factor | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Community Review & Critics Score | 30% |
| Cross-Platform Engagement | 20% |
| Active Fan Growth | 20% |
| Paid Messages | 15% |
| Gifts & Tips | 15% |

## Data Integrity Commitment

- No pay-for-placement
- Independent evaluation
- Continuous fraud monitoring
- Public methodology transparency

## Positioning

Youbi 1000 is:
- a credibility index
- an engagement ranking
- a fan-driven signal system

NOT:
- a follower leaderboard
- a paid ranking system
